# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most. Each question and answer is written to stand on
its own so the agent can retrieve exactly the one it needs.

## Billing Questions

**Can I get a refund if I forgot to cancel?**
If the charge was within the last 30 days on a monthly plan (14 days on annual) and the
subscription was active, you're likely eligible. Refunds under $100 are processed immediately
by the agent; $100–$500 need manager approval. Just ask in chat.

**Why was I charged twice?**
Duplicate charges are refundable under the "duplicate" reason. Give the agent the dates of
both charges and we'll refund the duplicate — usually on the spot if it's under $100.

**How much do the plans cost?**
Starter is $29/month, Pro is $79/month, and Enterprise is $299/month. Annual billing saves
20% and is non-refundable after 14 days.

**What happens if my payment fails?**
We automatically retry on days 3, 5, and 7 and email you each time. You keep access during
that window. Update your payment method anytime to retry immediately. See Payment Failure
Recovery.

## Account Questions

**How do I add a team member?**
Invite them from account settings. Starter supports up to 5 users, Pro up to 25 users, and
Enterprise is unlimited. Upgrade if you're at your limit.

**How do I cancel?**
Ask the agent or use billing settings. By default cancellation takes effect at the end of
your current period, so you keep access until then. Immediate cancellation is also available
but goes through a short approval step.

**What happens to my data if I cancel?**
We keep it for 90 days so you can reactivate. After 90 days it's permanently deleted.

## Technical Questions

**Does Pro include API access?**
Yes — Pro includes 10,000 API requests/month. Starter does not include API access at all.
Enterprise is unlimited.

**Why am I getting 429 errors from the API?**
You've hit your per-minute rate limit or your monthly quota. Check the rate-limit headers,
and upgrade to Enterprise for unlimited requests.

**What's your uptime guarantee?**
99.5% on Starter, 99.9% on Pro, and 99.99% on Enterprise. See the SLA and Uptime Policy for
how credits work.

**I want to dispute a charge — what should I do?**
Contact us first. A refund is far faster than a bank dispute (days versus months), and if
the charge is eligible we can usually resolve it immediately. Once a charge is disputed
through your bank, we can no longer refund it directly. See the Dispute and Chargeback Policy.
