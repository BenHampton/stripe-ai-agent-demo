# Refund Policy

We offer refunds on subscription charges under specific conditions. This document
defines eligibility criteria, approval requirements, and processing timelines for
all refund requests.

## Eligibility Criteria

A refund request is eligible if ALL of the following conditions are met:

- The charge occurred within the last **30 days**
- The subscription was active at the time of charge
- The customer has not previously received a refund for the same billing period
- The request is not related to disputed or fraudulent charges (those follow the Dispute Policy)

Requests that do not meet eligibility criteria will be declined and the customer
should be directed to our appeals process.

## Approval Thresholds

Refund amounts determine the approval workflow:

- **Under $100**: Agent may approve and process immediately, no human review required
- **$100 to $500**: Requires manager approval via the approval queue before processing
- **Over $500**: Requires senior finance review. Agent must escalate with full justification.

These thresholds apply to the refund amount, not the total subscription value.
A $299/month Enterprise customer requesting a $50 refund follows the under-$100 workflow.

## Prorated Refunds

When a customer cancels mid-cycle and requests a prorated refund:

1. Calculate the unused days remaining in the billing period
2. Divide the monthly charge by the number of days in the billing period
3. Multiply by unused days to get the prorated amount
4. Apply the standard approval threshold to the prorated amount

Example: $79/month Pro plan, cancelled with 15 days remaining in a 30-day period.
Prorated refund = ($79 / 30) * 15 = $39.50. This falls under the $100 threshold
and can be processed immediately.

## Processing Timeline

- Approved refunds are processed within 1 business day
- Funds appear on the customer credit card within 5–10 business days
- Customers receive an email confirmation when the refund is initiated

## What We Do Not Refund

- Charges older than 30 days
- Charges for usage-based add-ons already consumed
- Charges disputed through the customer's bank (contact Dispute team)
- Annual plan charges after 14 days of the billing date

## Refund Reasons

When processing a refund, the agent must record one of the following reasons:
- **duplicate**: Customer was charged twice for the same period
- **requested_by_customer**: Customer is dissatisfied and within eligibility window
- **service_issue**: Our platform caused a verifiable outage or data loss
